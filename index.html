Our Story

Midtown Grill first opened its doors in 2010 and quickly became known as the best steakhouse in Amsterdam. Inspired by the tradition of the quintessential American steakhouse, we serve mouthwatering steaks complemented by a curated wine list and craft cocktails. We also pride ourselves on our decadent desserts to finish off a great night.
